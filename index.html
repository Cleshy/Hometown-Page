Why Visit Sopron? Uncover the City’s Hidden Gems!

Step into History

Wander through the charming Old Town, where medieval streets and baroque architecture tell centuries-old stories. Visit the iconic Firewatch Tower for breathtaking views, and immerse yourself in the rich history of Sopron’s well-preserved buildings and hidden courtyards.

Photo by Vasily Nemchinov on Unsplash

Sip & Savor

Taste world-class wines in the heart of one of Hungary’s finest wine regions . Enjoy a glass of Kékfrankos while taking in the scenic vineyards, or pair local delicacies with exquisite flavors in traditional wine cellars that have stood the test of time.

Photo by Kelsey Knight on Unsplash

Nature at Your Doorstep

Explore breathtaking hiking trails, lush forests, and panoramic views at Lővérek and beyond. Whether you’re seeking a peaceful walk among ancient trees or an adventurous climb to scenic viewpoints, Sopron’s nature offers a refreshing escape in every season .

Photo by Juliane Liebermann on Unsplash

Experience Culture & Music

Immerse yourself in Sopron’s vibrant cultural scene with live music, open-air festivals, and classical concerts. From historical venues to modern performances, the city's artistic soul comes alive all year round .

Photo by Larisa Birta on Unsplash

Relax & Rejuvenate

Unwind in nearby thermal baths and wellness spas, where natural hot springs provide the perfect escape. Enjoy a soothing soak or indulge in a revitalizing massage after a long day of exploration.

Photo by Sven Mieke on Unsplash

Charming Café Moments

Take a break in Sopron’s cozy cafés, where rich Hungarian coffee and delightful pastries await. Sit back and soak in the atmosphere of the town’s historic streets while enjoying a fresh brew.
